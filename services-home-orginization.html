Home Organization

Do you have a space in your home that overwhelms you? Do you feel like your stuff is weighing you down? Do you wish your home was more organized, but you don’t know where to start or don’t have the time to do it?

We can help you to organize and simplify your home and create functional and attractive spaces that are designed for you and how your family functions. As a result, you will be able to:

  • easily find your personal belongings
  • maximize your space
  • reduce waste
  • reduce your stress
  • find peace and harmony in your home.

We will provide you with:

  • A thorough consultation to understand your needs and goals
  • Proposed solutions
  • Hands-on execution of solution plans
  • Shopping for any organizing products, if required
  • Removal of one car-load of items to be disposed or donated, per session
  • Coordination of additional donations and large-item disposal
  • Maintenance plans to help you stay organized after the initial work is done

 Areas organized:

  • Basements
  • Kitchens
  • Bathrooms
  • Family and Play Rooms
  • Entrance Halls and Mudrooms
  • Garages
  • Bedrooms and Closets
  • Home offices
